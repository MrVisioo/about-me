Candles Simulator

Candle making and burning simulator. Inspired by Let's create! Pottery this game adds it own twist allowing to destroy finished creations.

Candles simulator was made in collaboration with local candle artist and is used in cross-media marketing campaign.

Candles simulator is made in an overall low-fi aesthetic. UI design, low-poly models and low-resolution textures are used to bring a sense of home and nostalgia. A metaphor for the natural warmth of the candle.

Use your mouse cursor an a variety of settings to shape the candle to your liking. Save it for a rainy day or watch it burn.

Krab Island

Krab Island is an experience of living on a desert island. Unique art style and environment details are fun to look at while you hunt the crabs for a meal.

Krab Island soundtrack is instrumental ambient performed on sitar and singing bowl, which further amplifies the aesthetic.

Krab Island is an ode to the lazy hot summer day.

Durak Online

Durak-Online, was created in response to a need for a simple online platform for playing Durak card game. Other available solutions required complicated setup processes, so I took it upon myself to create a user-friendly option. With numerous successful Durak-Online sites currently in existence, there is certainly a market for it.

Although nothing in this project itself was particularly complex, the real-time nature of an online real-time game necessitated some shift in concepts. My focus was scalability and minimal latency.

The creation of the frontend for this project was an enjoyable experience. Having previously worked with Flutter, I chose to use it again. Durak Online being my biggest Flutter project yet, I'm satisfied with the competitive look and feel that I managed to design and create.

The Smith And The Devil

The Smith And The Devil is a game about the beauty of material science, of working with metal. A game about dark force lending the power to shape stone for the reason to subdue human soul into a place of suffering. Or maybe it was all just a dream... Brought from the toxic fumes of the blacksmith trade.

A high concept piece at the design stage The Smith And The Devil currently exists as a freely available demo.

War Of Kyborg

War Of Kyborg is an early experiment in game development and software development for me overall. It's inspired with ascii rogue-like titles such as ADOM and Dwarf Fortress.

War Of Kyborg is turn-based hot-sit two-player game. Each player takes control of a Kyborgtm (a robot really). The Kyborgs are powerful beings able to punch through rock, which is one of the game objectives, but they cannot swim in water.

Players must collect stone to build infrastructure: fortifications, roads, stairs. Everything that can help them defend against the inevitable enemy attack.

Developed on and off between years 2012-2016 it came through multiple iterations of complete rewrites in Pascal, Delphi and C++. Some fun features include the ability to weaponize infinite loops against the opponent.
